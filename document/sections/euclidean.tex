\section{Euclidean Geometry}



Notations for geometry:
\begin{align}
\skap{ v }{ u }_p  &:
\text{scalar product of two tangent vectors $ v $ and $ u $ at a point $ p $}
\end{align}
Simplified notations:
\begin{align}
\skap{ v }{ u }  &\equiv  \skap{ v }{ u }_p\\
\skaq{ v }       &\equiv  \skap{ v }{ v }
\end{align}
